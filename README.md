🎓 SkillBridge – Placement and Internship Portal
📌 Overview

SkillBridge is a full-stack web application designed to bridge the gap between students' skills and career opportunities. The platform helps students identify skill gaps, track their progress, and become placement-ready through personalized recommendations and faculty-led assessments.

🎯 Objectives
Monitor student skill development and academic progress.
Facilitate internship and placement readiness assessment.
Enable faculty to evaluate students and provide feedback.
Provide administrators with analytics and approval workflows.
Deliver real-time notifications and progress tracking.
